## Enclosure Notes

- Ornament shell: 3D printed
- Barrel jack: 5.5mm/2.1mm panel-mount, requires **11mm hole** in shell
- Orient jack on bottom â€” cable exits downward, follows ornament wire naturally
- Minimum 15mm clearance behind jack inside shell for solder tabs + wiring

## RF Workflow (per unit)

1. Run `Sniffer.ino` with receiver on pin 2 â€” press all remote buttons, record codes
2. Fill `RF_CODE_ON`, `RF_CODE_OFF`, `RF_BITS`, `RF_PROTOCOL` in Rev4
3. Flash Rev4, put outlet in learn mode, scan any RFID card to pair
4. Confirm outlet responds, ship pre-paired
